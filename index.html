Help & Workflow Guide

EXW Workflow (Step-by-Step)

  1. Receive readiness date from shipper.
  2. Two weeks before readiness, request cargo details & chamber attested documents.
  3. Send enquiry to local freight forwarder and receive quotes.
  4. Award shipment to selected forwarder.
  5. Confirm collection date and booking (flight/vessel).
  6. Confirm materials collected.
  7. Track departure, then update every 7 days (sea freight).
  8. Confirm arrival & delivery.

Use the navigation bar to switch between Dashboard, Add Shipment, Calendar, and Documents. Hover on info icons for tooltips.
